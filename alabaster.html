Alabaster Panels

Add an innovative edge to your interior design project with Metamore alabaster panels. Find a wide range of colours and finishes. Metamore Alabaster material is translucent in nature, the sheet serves as a diffused light source which can be used for both the interiors and the exteriors of different spaces. Alabaster is light weight yet looks like a marble stone when used tactfully.

  • Regency Alabaster
  • Onyx Alabaster
  • Travertine Alabaster
  • Artificial Alabaster
  • Amber Alabaster
  • Cloudy Alabaster
  • Trans Alabaster
  • Pearl Alabaster
