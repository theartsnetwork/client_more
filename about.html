About Metamore

Metamore is a global leading brand of Alabaster panel products for various industries. We manufacture dynamically designed translucent resin panels starting with Alabaster. You can select from the huge range of colour, pattern, texture and finished options and make your space truly delightful with Metamore panels. Our panels are all heat-formable, lesser-cutter friendly and available in standard sizes and thicknesses.

The Metamore is committed to producing the high-design panel products which are completely made in India. We are a pioneer in custom designed translucent panelling solutions in India, we are the first to introduce a throbbing rainbow of choices at a time when the industry consisted of only imported substandard quality Alabaster panels. We continue to expand our line of offerings with innovative, spectacular colours and patterns for our customers. We focus on delivering total operational excellence throughout the entire manufacturing process.

A spirit of cutting-edge design and passion for excellence drives everything that happens at Metamore. For architects and designers alike, the excitement lies in pushing the envelope on every new sheet and finding entirely new ways to look at panelling designs. The Metamore Industries assures the best quality, cost-effective, and high-end design solutions sought by commercial architects, designers for projects and applications across multiple industries throughout India.

The Metamore is always an innovation-focused company. We believe in creative exploration and endlessly mutable custom translucent panel products in the long term at the most cost-effective rate. The fresh colours and the organic lines of our Alabaster series, Metamore has a unique, handmade panelling solution to meet your needs.

Metamore is one of the leading global brands of custom translucent decorative panels, specifically Alabaster sold primarily into the architecture and design industries.
